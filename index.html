How to stay prepared as a D1 Volleyball Player

Wardrobe you SHOULD have:

  1. Nike Leggings/Spandex

    2. OR YOU CAN CHOOSE...


  2. DSG Leggings/Spandex
  3. A shirt or long sleeve that you are COMFORTABLE in and are able to MOVE in

Gear you SHOULD have:

  1. Volleyball or basketball shoes

  2. Kneepads (perferably Underarmour or Nike)

  3. Arm sleeves or Elbow pads (Optional but helpful)

  4. Finger tape (Optional)

  5. Water bottles are always a MUST! Stay hydrated
